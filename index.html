Product Technicals

NRC stands for Noise Reduction Coefficient. Our 0.85+ rating means 85% or more of sound energy hitting the panel is absorbed rather than reflected — a professional studio standard.
They are Class A fire-rated — the highest safety classification for interior building materials. They self-extinguish and severely limit flame spread, meeting the strictest interior safety codes.
No. Our PET fiber is 100% odorless and VOC-free. Unlike many foam products, there is absolutely zero off-gassing — safe for any enclosed studio or bedroom environment.
Each panel is 12mm thick — the ideal balance between acoustic performance (strong mid-to-high frequency absorption) and a low-profile, sleek wall presence.
Yes. They can be easily and cleanly trimmed with a standard utility knife or sharp scissors. Score the felt surface, then snap for a clean straight edge.
Hextile excels at absorbing mid-to-high frequencies (500Hz–8kHz). For heavy sub-bass control below 200Hz, we recommend layering panels in corners or supplementing with dedicated corner bass traps.
100% recycled polyester fiber (PET) — the same material used in high-end acoustic felt worldwide. Sustainable, durable, and fully recyclable at end of life with no landfill impact.
The luxury felt finish is anti-static and naturally resists significant dust buildup, keeping maintenance to a minimum in a typical studio or home environment.
A quick vacuum with a brush attachment or a lightly damp cloth is all you need. Avoid machine washing or harsh chemical cleaners that could break down the fiber structure.
We don't recommend painting as it clogs the fiber surface pores and can significantly reduce sound absorption performance. The tiles come in professional colorways designed to need no painting.

Installation & Usage

When used with removable 3M adhesive strips, they are completely renter-friendly. They peel off cleanly without leaving marks or residue on standard painted drywall.
Absolutely not. The DIY friction-fit design is built for solo home setup. Most full installations are completed in under 30 minutes with zero tools required.
Yes. The panels are lightweight enough for secure ceiling mounting with appropriate adhesive strips. Ceiling treatment is especially effective for eliminating flutter echo in home theaters and vocal rooms.
Start by drawing or applying a horizontal level-line across the center of your target wall. The precision geometric edges of each tile naturally guide all subsequent placement — no measuring needed after that.
Absolutely. Hextile panels are ideal for improving dialogue clarity and surround-sound accuracy in home cinema setups, reducing unwanted reflections from hard walls and ceilings.
These panels are designed for internal sound absorption (improving acoustics within your room), not soundproofing (blocking sound through walls). For soundproofing, additional mass-loaded vinyl and decoupling are required.
Any smooth, clean surface works — including drywall, wood paneling, painted concrete, and glass. Ensure the surface is dry and dust-free before applying adhesive strips for maximum hold.
Yes. If using removable adhesive strips, you can peel the tiles off cleanly and re-stick them in a new position. The felt surface holds up well through multiple repositioning cycles.
