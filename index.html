Product & Performance

NRC stands for Noise Reduction Coefficient. A rating of 0.9 means the panels absorb 90% of sound energy that hits them — only 10% is reflected back into the room. This is professional studio standard performance.
The Soundsbay Honeycomb panels use a unique honeycomb PET structure. TONOR Hexagon panels are high-density 1700g/m² hex tiles. TONOR Square panels offer a clean minimalist grid look. The TONOR Wood panels add a luxury real wood veneer finish for premium interiors. All achieve 0.9 NRC performance.
No. All panel products use self-adhesive or removable adhesive systems designed to peel off cleanly without damage. They are 100% renter-friendly on standard painted drywall.
Yes. All panels are made from 100% non-toxic, odorless polyester fiber. They have no sharp edges and are completely safe for households with children and pets.
Yes — the PET fiber panels (Products 1, 2, 4) are lightweight enough for ceiling mounting with appropriate adhesive strips. The Wood panels (Product 3) include screws for secure ceiling installation.
These panels are designed for sound absorption within your room (reducing echo and reverberation), not for blocking sound transmission through walls. For sound blocking, you'd also need mass-loaded vinyl and wall decoupling.
Our panels excel at mid-to-high frequencies (500Hz–8kHz+). For heavy bass control below 200Hz, we recommend layering panels in corners or adding dedicated bass traps.
Any smooth, clean, dry surface — drywall, wood, painted concrete, glass, metal, and ceramic tiles. Clean the surface before applying for maximum adhesion.

FIXCHORD Tape

For most PET fiber panels (30×30cm), the 1.5cm or 2cm width gives the best balance of hold strength and ease of removal. For heavier wooden panels, use the 2cm or 2.5cm width for a more secure bond.
The FIXCHORD tape is designed for permanent bonding. Once applied, it creates an industrial-strength hold that is not intended to be repositioned. For removable applications, use 3M Command strips instead.
Yes. The FIXCHORD 3M VHB tape is UV-resistant, waterproof, and rated for outdoor use. It maintains adhesion in both extreme heat and cold conditions.
The tape is ideal for LED strip lights, car trim, home décor mounting, cable management, mirror mounting, and any bonding application where drilling is not possible or desired.
